# Restaurant OS - Project Context

## About the Project

Restaurant OS is a Restaurant Operating System for India.

The goal is to automate restaurant operations and simplify business management for owners and investors.

Initially focused on restaurants and cafes, with future expansion into hotels and multi-branch hospitality businesses.
